Jamaica Bay

Water Quality 2019

Much of New York City has a combined sewer system. Sewage from homes and storm drains travel in the same pipes and are treated together. When there is too much flow to treat, like during a rainstorm, overflow is diverted into local waterways through combined sewer overflow pipes.

The overflow pours into the tidal estuaries surrounding Jamaica Bay. This causes high levels of bacteria, particulary in the summer months.
